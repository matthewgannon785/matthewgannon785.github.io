Assignment 1

In this assignment, I perform a 2 part analysis and use a variety of rtools for analysis including the following:

Part 1: Analysis of flight data from JFK to LAX.

  • Creating a frequency table and barplot of flights by carrier.
  • Calculating average arrival delay for each carrier and creating density plots.
  • Generating a scatterplot of departure versus arrival delay by carrier.
  • Mapping the frequency of flights to different destinations.

Part 2: Analysis of maths education data.

  • Correlation Plot: Visualizing correlations between variables related to maths achievement.
  • Gender Analysis: Investigating the role of gender in maths achievement scores using boxplots.
  • Big Five Personality Factors Regression: Fitting a linear regression model with maths achievement as the response variable and scores of big five personality factors as explanatory variables.
  • The model's summary and coefficient distributions are examined.
