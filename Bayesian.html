Assignment 2

The goal of Assignment 2 is to create a scientific report utilizing Bayesian Workflow, Classification, and Hierarchical GLM to model a dataset on repeat rates in Thai primary schools. The report must include Bayesian logistic regression using variational inference with the Jaakkola and Jordan bound, compared with results from Stan/rstanarm. The dataset, educ.RData, contains information on repeat rates, gender, pre-primary education, and mean pupil socio-economic status at the school level.
