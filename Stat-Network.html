Assignment 1

In this assignment, I carry out the following tasks:

  1. Calculate Ireland's weighted degree and identify the top 5 trading partners.
  2. Construct an undirected binary adjacency matrix representing oil trading relationships in 2019. Identify the 10 countries with the highest degrees in this network.
  3. Visualize the network with node sizes determined by betweenness centrality, excluding nodes with zero degree.
  4. Develop a custom function to compute the 22-core set on the network and compare the results with the igraph function.
  5. Analyze whether the network exhibits a power law degree distribution and assortative or disassortative mixing based on degrees.

Assignment 2

In this assignment, I carry out the following tasks:

  1. Perform spectral clustering on X and visualize the network with node colors representing clusters and comment on the geographical relation of clusters.
  2. Fit a stochastic block model to X and interpret the resulting groups.
  3. Fit a 2-dimensional Latent Position Model to X and visualize the latent space.
  4. Simulate 100 random networks from the fitted model and compare their density with the observed density in X.
