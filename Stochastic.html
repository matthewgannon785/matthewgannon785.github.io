Kaplan-Meier / Cox Proportional-HazardS / Log Rank Test / Latent Class Analysis / Cluster Analysis

Assignment 1

The data on the survival times of 65 multiple myeloma patients were recorded. This assignment asked me to:

1. Construct Kaplan-Meier estimates of the time-to-death survival functions for patients with and without abnormal platelets at diagnosis. Estimate and compare the median time-to-death for patients from those two groups.

2. Compare the estimated survival time distributions of the two groups using a log-rank test.

3. Use the Cox proportional hazards model to study the impact both platelets and other patient covariates on the time-to-death and develop a parsimonious model. Interpret the model fit.

Assignment 2

The House of Commons in the UK held a series of indicative votes on Britain’s with- drawal from the EU (Brexit) on March 27th and April 1st, 2019.

This assignment asked me to use the latent class analysis models to answer the following questions:

1. Do the House of Commons politicians fall into clusters when voting on Brexit, where the politicians in each cluster tend to have similar voting behaviour?

2. How many clusters are there? Justify and provide summaries of the clusters that you find.

Subsequently, I perform an analysis of a website’s clickstream data and, using transition matrices and cluster analysis, provide a detailed probabilistic description of how users navigate the site.
