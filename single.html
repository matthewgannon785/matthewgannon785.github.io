Assignment 1

In this assignment, I perform a manual forward propagation calculation through a defined neural network and manually calculate the loss associated with this training instance using an appropriate loss function. Next I perform an investigation of data concerning electroencephalography (EEG) measurements of a sample composed by patients with epilepsy and healthy subjects. The task was to predict the type of EEG activity using the signal features recorded under different conditions and different areas of the brain and answer the following questions.

1. Deploy a neural network with 2 hidden layers and a neural network with 3 hidden layers. Compare appropriately the performances of these two networks at predicting the type of condition (and area of the brain) an EEG signal belongs to.

2. Does adding one extra hidden layer to the network architecture lead to an improvement in the predictive performance?

3. Evaluate the test accuracy of the two deep neural networks and comment briefly on their performance. Which of the two networks provide the best accuracy at predicting EEG signals of patients with tumor formations on the test data?
